About JubileeVerse

Deepening faith through meaningful AI-powered conversations

Our Mission

JubileeVerse was born from a simple yet profound vision: to make spiritual guidance, biblical scholarship, and faith-based conversations accessible to everyone, everywhere, at any time.

In the spirit of the biblical Jubilee, a time of restoration, freedom, and new beginnings, we believe that technology can serve as a bridge to deeper understanding and spiritual growth. Our platform combines advanced AI with rich theological knowledge to create meaningful conversations that nurture faith.

What We Believe

Faith First

Every feature and conversation is designed with reverence for scripture and respect for diverse Christian traditions.

Accessible Wisdom

Biblical scholarship and spiritual guidance should be available to all, regardless of location or resources.

Thoughtful Technology

AI serves as a tool for exploration and learning, complementing, not replacing, human spiritual community.

Global Reach

The Gospel transcends language barriers. Our multilingual support brings scripture to hearts worldwide.

The Name "JubileeVerse"

Our name draws inspiration from the Year of Jubilee described in Leviticus 25, a time when debts were forgiven, slaves were freed, and the land returned to its original owners. It was a year of restoration and new beginnings.

"Consecrate the fiftieth year and proclaim liberty throughout the land to all its inhabitants. It shall be a jubilee for you." - Leviticus 25:10

"Verse" represents both the sacred verses of scripture and the conversational verse, the back-and-forth dialogue that deepens understanding. Together, JubileeVerse embodies our commitment to restoration through the Word.

Our Approach

Each AI persona on JubileeVerse is carefully designed with specific expertise and personality. Drawing from vast theological resources stored in our vector database, our personas can engage in substantive conversations about scripture, prayer, worship, counseling, and more.

We believe in the power of conversation to transform understanding. Whether you're exploring the depths of Hebrew poetry, seeking prayer support, or learning to apply scripture to daily life, our personas are here to walk alongside you.
